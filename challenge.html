4 bucket-list activities in Thailand

1. Island Hoping

Thailand is famous for its magnificent islands like Koh Phi Phi (pictured above) and Koh Samui. I would love to visit these islands and enjoy the beautiful beaches and the crystal clear waters. The pictures on these beaches would also be to die for!

2. Visit the Grand Palace

The Grand Palace is a complex of buildings at the heart of Bangkok, Thailand. The palace has been the official residence of the Kings of Siam since 1782. Let's visit this place and learn more about the beautiful history of Thailand and it's people.

3. Visit the Floating Markets

Thailand is famous for its floating markets. I would love to visit the floating markets and enjoy the local food and culture.
Thailand is famous for its local cuisines so it would be a shame not to try the various dishes made by locals in these markets.

4. Visit the Tiger Temple

The Tiger Temple is a Buddhist temple in western Thailand that was founded in 1994 as a forest temple and sanctuary for wild animals, among them several tigers, the majority of which are Indochinese tigers. Wouldn't you like tointeract with the tigers?
